About Us

Burbank Oaks Apartments has been a home for many in the Sherman Oaks area for more than 30 years. We believe in apartments being more than just dwellings, and we hope that you can find a way to make yourself a part of the community we have built. Common areas are lined with trees and flower beds, and our gated pool is surrounded by modern furniture. Each unit recieves a spot in our gated parking lot. With more than 150 studio and one bedroom units, two on-site managers, and a team of highly motivated employees working to make you comfortable, we are sure that you won't regret submitting an application. Hope to see you soon!
